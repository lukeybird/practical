Essential Christian Concepts

Learn the fundamental truths of Christianity through interactive lessons

God's Love and Creation

God created everything out of love and made humans in His image.

  • God is the Creator of everything
  • Humans are made in God's image
  • God's love is unconditional
  • Creation reflects God's glory
"For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son..." - John 3:16

The Fall of Man and Sin

Through Adam and Eve's disobedience, sin entered the world.

  • Original sin affects all humanity
  • Sin separates us from God
  • God's law reveals our sin
  • We need a Savior
"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God" - Romans 3:23

Jesus Christ

God's Son who came to earth, died for our sins, and rose again.

  • Jesus is fully God and fully man
  • He lived a perfect life
  • He died for our sins
  • He rose from the dead
"I am the way, and the truth, and the life" - John 14:6

Salvation through Faith

We are saved by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.

  • Salvation is a gift from God
  • We can't earn salvation
  • Faith in Jesus saves us
  • Good works follow salvation
"For by grace you have been saved through faith" - Ephesians 2:8

The Holy Spirit

The third person of the Trinity who indwells believers and empowers them.

  • The Holy Spirit is God
  • He indwells all believers
  • He convicts of sin
  • He produces spiritual fruit
"But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control" - Galatians 5:22-23

The Bible

God's inspired and authoritative Word given to guide and instruct believers.

  • The Bible is God's Word
  • It is inspired and inerrant
  • It is our authority for faith and practice
  • It reveals God's character and plan
"All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness" - 2 Timothy 3:16

Prayer

Communication with God that builds relationship and brings His will to earth.

  • Prayer is conversation with God
  • Jesus modeled prayer for us
  • Prayer includes praise, confession, thanksgiving, and supplication
  • God answers prayer according to His will
"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God" - Philippians 4:6

The Church

The community of believers united by faith in Christ, called to worship and serve together.

  • The Church is the body of Christ
  • Believers are called to gather together
  • The Church is built on Christ as the foundation
  • The Church has a mission to make disciples
"And I tell you that you are Peter, and on this rock I will build my church, and the gates of Hades will not overcome it" - Matthew 16:18
